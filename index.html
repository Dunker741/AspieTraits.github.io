Aspen Traits

At Aspen Star Base Academy, we strive to create a truly revolutionary and future-based learning experience. This means we will work with our student Aspens not only on their academic skills, but their behavioral skills as well. We believe that this more comprehensive approach will benefit students exponentially more than traditional education systems.

One way we plan to do this is by recognizing common Aspen traits, isolating them, and dealing with them accordingly. We have seperated 53 of the most common Aspen traits into 3 different categories. A green trait is a positive trait we want to not only keep, but promote. A yellow trait is neutral. It can be good or bad depending on the situation. Or perhaps it's a negative trait that can't be entirely eliminated. Therefore we want to control or modify it as much as possible. A red trait is a negative trait that tends to hold Aspens back in life. These are traits we will seek to eliminate or at least minimize.
